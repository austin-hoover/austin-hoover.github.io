I'm currently completing a PhD in physics from the [University of Tennessee, Knoxille](http://physics.utk.edu), studying high-intensity proton beam dynamics as part of the Accelerator Physics group at the [Spallation Neutron Source](https://neutrons.ornl.gov/sns) (SNS). I previously obtained a BS in physics from [Wheaton College](https://www.wheaton.edu). 

I've created a research/technical blog [here](https://austin-hoover.github.io/blog/) which is focused mostly on physics, but also on other areas which interest me such as machine learning.

Outside of STEM, I enjoy learning about philosophy and sometimes post my thoughts [here](https://austin-hoover.github.io/thoughts/). In my free time, I sometimes play chess (badly) or piano (badly).
